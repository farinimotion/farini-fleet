FAQ

Questions investors ask us first.

How do returns work?
Each vehicle category has an indicative return profile based on projected daily usage. Once your vehicle joins the fleet and is assigned a driver, we track its income and remit your share on a fixed schedule.
Who drives the vehicle?
A driver vetted and trained by our team. Drivers are background-checked, onboarded against our service standards, and monitored for performance while operating fleet vehicles.
What happens if the vehicle is damaged?
Fleet vehicles are insured against accident and damage. If an incident occurs, our operations team handles the claim and repair, and keeps you informed throughout.
Can I choose my own driver?
Drivers are assigned by our vetting team to keep quality and accountability consistent across the fleet. You're welcome to refer a candidate, and we'll run them through the same screening process.
How long is the investment period?
Terms vary by vehicle category and are confirmed with you before your vehicle joins the fleet. Our team will walk you through the specific timeline that applies to your investment.
Is my investment insured?
The vehicle itself is insured against accident and damage. As with any income-generating asset, returns depend on usage and are not guaranteed — our team can walk you through the specifics.
